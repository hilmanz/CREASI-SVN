Welcome to CREASI

CREASI is the first online job search platform especially designed for the creative industry in Indonesia. Unlike other job search websites that cater more towards conventional office jobs, CREASI caters to exciting dynamic creative jobs.

We are here to support all the Creative Talents out there by magnifying their opportunity to maximize their potential talent. On the other side, we want every Talent Seekers to have a quicker and easier way to find the right talent for their next projects. We literally connect Creative Talents and Talent Seekers from Sabang to Merauke. Wohooo!!

Our mission is to discover and expose all Creative Talents in Indonesia to the local industries and ultimately to the world.

How It Works

If you are a creative talent, you can create your profile and build portfolio to expose your talent. You also have the privilege to apply to available jobs or projects posted by the talent seekers. For you talent seekers, more than just posting job and waiting, you have the luxury to browse creative talents from our broad creative categories. Anywhere and anytime!
